[Name]
Power_Supply
 
[Description]
The Power_Supply component controls how much energy is
allocated to the other components.
 
[Functionality]
Power_Supply processes commands in two(2) digit chunks.
 
The first(1) digit is the address of the component whose
energy is being adjusted.
 
The second(2) digit is the desired energy level for said
component.
 
Power_Supply can handle multiple two(2) digit chunks in
 a single command.
